About Us

Puglight is a student organization dedicated to breeding, entertaining and training pugs. We have been bringing joy to your beloved pets for over 5 years! Every week we have fun events to improve your pets' spirits and increase their physical abilities. Also, if you, like us, love animals very much, you can contact us and join our friendly team!
